The Daily Cycle

Precision logistics optimized for the restaurant clock.

01

Sign Up & Verification

Submit your restaurant details. Our operations team visits your location to verify and set up your credit limit.

02

Order Taking (9 PM - 1 AM)

Our dedicated Order Taker visits or calls you daily during closing hours to finalize requirements for the next day.

03

Dawn Delivery

Before your kitchen opens, our logistics fleet delivers fresh raw materials directly to your storage.

04

Payment

Pay Cash on Delivery for wholesale rates, or sign the receiving slip to utilize your 24-hour credit (+3.5%).
