Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

Freshly ("we," "us," or "our"), a project of ABCD Venture, is committed to protecting the privacy of our restaurant partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your operational and financial data.

1. Information We Collect

To provide credit facilities and logistics services, we collect:

  • Business Information: Restaurant name, location coordinates, tax identification (NTN), and ownership details.
  • Operational Data: Daily consumption patterns, ordering history, and preferred delivery windows.
  • Financial Information: Credit history (for 24h credit facility assessment) and payment records.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for operational efficiency:

  • Route Optimization: Delivery locations are used to plan logistics fleets for dawn deliveries.
  • Credit Assessment: Payment history determines your eligibility for the 24-hour credit facility and credit limit increases.
  • Inventory Planning: Ordering patterns help us stock fresh produce to minimize waste.

3. Data Protection & Sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. Data is only shared with:

  • Internal logistics teams for delivery execution.
  • Financial auditors (anonymized data only).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

4. Security

We employ enterprise-grade encryption for all digital records. Physical copies of invoices and credit agreements are stored in secure facilities with restricted access.

5. Contact Us

For data inquiries, please contact our operations team directly or email operations@freshly.com.
